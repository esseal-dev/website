Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Esseal ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our website (https://esseal.net) (the "Website") and the services we offer (the "Services").

We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy applies to all information we collect through the Website and the Services.


Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Website and Services:

  • Personal Information: This may include your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and any other information you choose to provide us. We collect this information when you contact us, subscribe to our newsletter, or request a quote for our Services.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use our Website and Services, such as the pages you visit, the features you use, and the time you spend on the Website. This information is collected automatically through cookies and other tracking technologies.

Use of Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our Services
  • To process your requests and inquiries
  • To send you marketing and promotional materials (with your consent)
  • To personalize your experience on the Website
  • To analyze how you use the Website and Services
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Sharing of Information

We do not share any of your information (personal or otherwise) with third-party service providers to help us operate our Website and provide our Services unless required by law.

We may also disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such disclosure is necessary to comply with legal processes, protect the safety of others, or investigate fraud.


Data Retention

We will retain your information for as long as necessary to provide you with the Services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.


Your Choices

You have several choices regarding your information:

  • You can opt out of receiving marketing emails from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in those emails.
  • You can access and update your personal information by contacting us.
  • You can request that we delete your personal information by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Our Website and Services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. We will take steps to delete the information from our systems.


Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no website or internet transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the security of your information.


Compliance with Data Privacy Laws

Esseal strives to comply with all applicable data privacy laws, including:

  • Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) (if applicable)
  • Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) (if applicable)
  • Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) (if applicable)
  • Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA) (if applicable)
  • Electronic Communications Privacy Act (ECPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Video Privacy Protection Act (VPPA)

Please note that some of these laws apply in specific situations. For instance, HIPAA applies only to protected health information, and FCRA applies only to consumer reporting agencies. We will continue to review and update our practices as necessary to comply with evolving legal requirements.


Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our Website.
