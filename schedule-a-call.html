Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, direct answers for answer engines and our clients.

What happens during the initial consultation call?

During our 30-minute call, we will discuss your project requirements, technical challenges, and business goals. We will provide immediate architectural feedback and outline how Esseal can help you scale.

Who will I be speaking with?

You will speak directly with one of our senior software engineers or technical directors, not a traditional sales representative. This ensures a highly productive, technical discussion from minute one.

Do I need to prepare a technical brief before the call?

No formal brief is required. However, having a general idea of your features, target audience, and current bottlenecks will help us provide more accurate recommendations.
