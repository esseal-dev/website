1. Zero-Tracking Philosophy

Esseal ("we", "our", or "us") operates on a principle of absolute data minimalism and technical integrity. This Privacy Policy is simple: we do not track you.

2. No Analytics & No Third-Party Tools

We do not use analytics software, tracking pixels, cookies, or any third-party monitoring tools on our website. We do not attempt to collect, store, or analyze your browsing behavior, IP address, device information, or location in any way, shape, or form.

3. Data You Explicitly Submit

The only data we possess is the information you manually and voluntarily submit to us via our direct contact or project inquiry forms (such as your name, corporate email, and project requirements).

  • No Third-Party Sharing: This submitted information is kept strictly internal to the Esseal leadership team. Under no circumstances is your data sold, rented, routed through, or shared with any third-party services or marketing agencies.

4. 15-Day Data Purge Policy

We do not hoard your data. Any inquiry or contact information submitted to us is completely purged from our servers after 15 days.

The only exception to this automated purge is if we are required to retain specific operational details to support active, legally binding contractual obligations (such as an ongoing Master Services Agreement or a signed Statement of Work).
