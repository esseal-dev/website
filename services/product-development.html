Partnership FAQs

How does your equity partnership model work?

We selectively partner with visionary founders. If our engineering leadership evaluates your product idea and sees significant market potential, we may offer to build your product at a heavily subsidized cash rate in exchange for a mutually agreed-upon equity stake in your startup. We essentially become your acting technical co-founders.

I have an idea but no technical background. Can you help?

Absolutely. That is exactly who this service is built for. We bridge the gap between your industry expertise and the technical execution. We handle the entire technology stack—from database architecture to cloud hosting—while continuously explaining the "why" behind our technical decisions in plain, business-focused language.

What happens after the product is launched?

A launch is just the beginning. Unlike standard agencies that disappear post-delivery, we establish long-term relationships. We monitor production performance, implement user analytics, and continuously iterate on the codebase based on real user feedback to help you achieve product-market fit.
