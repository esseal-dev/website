Operational FAQs

Should we buy an existing SaaS ERP or build a custom one?

If an existing SaaS product matches your workflow perfectly and licensing costs are low, you should buy it. However, most construction, logistics, and manufacturing companies have highly unique, specialized processes. Buying SaaS often forces them into expensive "workarounds." Building a custom ERP with Esseal ensures the software bends to your business, and you eliminate recurring per-user licensing fees entirely.

How do you transition a non-technical team from Excel to a web app?

Adoption is the biggest hurdle in internal tools. We solve this by heavily investing in intuitive UX and strictly mapping the new application to their existing mental models. We don't overcomplicate the interface; we build familiar data grids, logical step-by-step forms, and provide robust training to ensure a frictionless transition from spreadsheets to the new system.

Which industries do you have the most experience mapping workflows for?

Our senior engineers have extensive experience mapping and digitizing complex physical workflows for the Construction sector, large-scale NGOs, Manufacturing factories, Logistics and supply chain companies, and the Telecom industry. We excel at translating physical operations into scalable data models.
