The Esseal Engineering Philosophy

Whether we are giving back to the developer community with open-source components, or deploying massive internal ERPs for our enterprise clients, our architectural rules never change.

  • No Utility Bloat: We strictly write pure vanilla CSS. No Tailwind, no heavy UI frameworks.
  • Strictly Typed: All logic is governed by strict TypeScript to eliminate runtime errors.
  • Ownership: When we deploy an internal system for you, you own the IP. No per-seat licenses.
